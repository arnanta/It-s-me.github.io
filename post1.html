Rent-a-Car

This was a project I started back in April 2020 when I first learned React, node js. I was the sole creator/developer for the project and learnt things along the way.

The system contains web pages where we can choose our car depending on the city we are living in. Then we can rent it.

Another pages were there for login and registration separately. One can login if he/she has registered from before.

Customers get attractive discounts after certain periods of time. They can rent a car even for days in the cheapest rate.

I worked on this system for a few days.
